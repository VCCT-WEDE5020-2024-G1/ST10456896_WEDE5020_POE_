Mission

The mission of Precious Radebe SME initiative is to empower domestic workers and gardeners by providing them with essential skills training that enhances their employability, increases their earning potential, and equips them with the knowledge and abilities to start their own small businesses. Through this initiative, she aims to uplift members of her community who did not have the opportunity to pursue formal education or upskilling, thereby creating a more skilled and marketable workforce.

About Us
